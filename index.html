Plainpage — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Plainpage does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All documents you create in Plainpage are stored locally on your own device (or in a folder you choose). Plainpage does not use analytics, does not connect to any server, does not require an account, and does not share any information with third parties.

Because nothing is collected, there is nothing to request, export, or delete on our end — your data never leaves your device unless you choose to export or share it yourself.

If this policy ever changes, the update will be posted on this page.
